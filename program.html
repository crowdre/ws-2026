The 10th International Workshop on Crowd-Based Requirements Engineering (CrowdRE'26)



TBD, 2026
In conjunction with RE 2026

Program

CrowdRE'26 is co-located with the RE 2026 conference. The workshop intends to promote lively discussions among participants from both industry and academia.

Workshop Format

CrowdRE'26 is intended as a one-day workshop held before the RE'26 main conference. This celebratory tenth edition will feature:

  • Keynote session — An invited keynote speaker will present on a topic relevant to CrowdRE
  • 10-year celebratory session — A dedicated session for reflecting on this milestone through talks and a history of the workshop series
  • Paper sessions — Two paper sessions with up to three paper presentations each. Paper presentations may last up to 20 minutes and are followed by a discussant who puts the paper into scientific perspective, lists contributions, and raises issues and questions for discussion
  • Interactive session — Specifically planned to discuss and elaborate the roadmap for CrowdRE that we intend to publish as a whitepaper or peer-reviewed publication

Detailed Program

The detailed program will be announced after paper acceptance notifications.
